## Hobbyt
Nowadays everyone has many hobbies but not enough time to find the right company to do it with.
Hobbyt lets you find people on the fly to join the fun.
It doesn't matter if you want to find new friends for a relaxing evening at home or a group of ninja turtles to go get the bad guys!

Hobbyt got you covered!

with a quick and simple register login and advertising systems.
you can find the right group to do the next thing you want to do in the nick of time!
so don't let loneliness get the best for you, just hop in and do what you love anywhere anytime and with the right company!

## Tutorial
Register to the site with a username and password.
Log in to the site.
Add the activity you're doing and how many people you're looking for to join you.
View all events from the list and join one of them if you like.
To delete an activity, click on the trash icon next to it.
To update an activity, click on the edit button.
